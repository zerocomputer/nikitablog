# What I'm Reading in 2026

Books are one of my main sources of ideas and inspiration. Here's what I'm reading this year.

## Technical

- **Designing Data-Intensive Applications** — Martin Kleppmann
- **Staff Engineer** — Will Larson

## Business & Management

- **Turn the Ship Around!** — L. David Marquet
- **The Mom Test** — Rob Fitzpatrick

## Fiction

- **Dune** — Frank Herbert (re-reading)

## What These Books Gave Me

Each of these books gave me a new perspective on some aspect of work and life. I especially recommend *The Mom Test* — it completely changed my approach to client communication.
